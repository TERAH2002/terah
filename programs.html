OUR PROGRAMS


Shelter and Housing Assistance: Providing temporary or permanent housing to individuals experiencing homelessness, or offering rental assistance or mortgage assistance for those at risk of becoming homeless.


Food Assistance: Providing food and meals to those in need through partnerships with local food banks and community organizations.

Medical and Mental Health Services: Offering access to medical and mental health services to individuals experiencing homelessness, including preventative care and treatment for medical and mental health issues.

Job Training and Employment Assistance: Providing job training and employment assistance to help individuals experiencing homelessness gain the skills and resources necessary to secure stable employment.

Education and Training Programs: Offering educational and training programs to help individuals experiencing homelessness develop new skills and improve their employment prospects.

Financial Literacy and Budgeting Programs: Offering financial literacy and budgeting programs to help individuals experiencing homelessness manage their finances and work towards financial stability.

Outreach and Awareness Programs: Raising awareness about the issue of homelessness and advocating for policies and programs that support individuals experiencing homelessness.
