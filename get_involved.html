Get Involved

At Homeless Hope Foundation, we believe that everyone can make a difference in the lives of those experiencing homelessness. Here are some ways you can get involved in our initiatives and projects:

**Volunteer Opportunities**
Homeless Hope Foundation offers a variety of volunteer opportunities to fit different interests and skill sets. Here are some examples of volunteer positions we currently have available:

1. Meal Service: Help prepare and serve meals at our shelter or mobile food truck. This position is perfect for individuals who enjoy cooking or have experience in the food service industry.

2. Outreach: Assist our outreach team in connecting with individuals who are experiencing homelessness in our community. This position involves going out into the community to distribute food, clothing, and other essential items.

3. Fundraising: Help us plan and execute fundraising events to support our programs and services. This position is ideal for individuals with experience in event planning, marketing, or fundraising.

4. Administrative Support: Assist with data entry, phone calls, and other administrative tasks at our office. This position is great for individuals who are organized and detail-oriented.

5. Tutoring and Mentoring: Help individuals experiencing homelessness improve their literacy, math, or other skills. This position is ideal for individuals with a background in education or tutoring.

6. Maintenance and Repairs: Assist with maintenance and repairs at our shelter or other facilities. This position is perfect for individuals with experience in construction, plumbing, or electrical work.

Volunteers can choose to work in one or more of these areas, depending on their interests and availability. We also offer flexible scheduling and can accommodate individuals who want to volunteer on a regular or occasional basis.



1. Visit our website and navigate to the "Get Involved" section.
2. Click on the "get involved" link.
3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click on the "Apply Now" button. This will take you to our online volunteer application form.
4. Fill out the application form with your personal information, availability, and interests.
5. Submit the application form by clicking on the "Submit" button.
6. Our volunteer coordinator will review your application and contact you within a week to schedule an interview.
7. During the interview, you will have the opportunity to learn more about our organization and discuss your interests and skills.
8. If you are selected to volunteer, you will be required to complete a background check and attend an orientation session before you can start volunteering.

We encourage all volunteers to commit to at least 4 hours of service per month. Our volunteer opportunities are flexible and can accommodate a variety of schedules. If you have any questions about the volunteer application process or our volunteer opportunities, please don't hesitate to contact us.
